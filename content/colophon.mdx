## Development

This site is built with [Next.js](https://nextjs.org), styled with [Tailwindcss](https://tailwindcss.com), hosted on [Vercel](https://vercel.com), the data and content is pulled from [Github](https://github.com/yuchengkuo/yckuo), [Spotify](https://spotify.com/user/cheng-90), [Last.fm](https://last.fm) and [Raindrop.io](https://raindrop.io)

## Typography

Typefaces that are used on this site,

- [Apfel Grotezk](http://collletttivo.it/) from collletttivo, for headings
- [Satoshi](https://www.fontshare.com/fonts/satoshi) from Fontshare, for body text
- [Spectral](https://spectral.productiontype.com/) from Production Type, used for highlighted text and blockquotes
